Portfolio Details

Summary of projects focused on customer behavior, business strategy, market trends, and environmental data.

The projects presented here were developed with diverse objectives and purposes, reflecting a wide range of data analysis skills and business applications. Each case addresses a unique problem, combining data insights with strategic thinking.

For example, in the Instacart project, the goal was to understand customer behavior and improve the efficiency of online grocery orders. The Rockbuster project focused on analyzing movie rental trends to guide content strategy and regional investments.

Data is only useful when it leads to action, my role is to bridge the gap between information and impact.


The Airbnb Amsterdam analysis aimed to uncover pricing patterns and factors influencing listing popularity in the short-term rental market. Lastly, the ClimateWins project explored climate data over several decades to identify trends and support the development of predictive models.

Across all projects, I used tools such as Python, SQL, Excel, and Tableau to clean, analyze, and visualize data. These experiences allowed me to apply technical skills in real-world contexts while developing insights that support data-driven decision-making.
